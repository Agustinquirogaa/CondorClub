The Club

The Condor Club is a boutique luxury experience company founded five years ago with a singular mission: to create unparalleled adventures for an exclusive community of discerning individuals.

We operate under a strict membership model with limited annual admissions, ensuring the highest standards of exclusivity and personalized service. Our carefully curated experiences are designed for those who seek not just luxury, but legacy.

With a maximum of 50 new members per year and rigorous entry requirements, we maintain the intimate atmosphere that defines true exclusivity. Each experience is crafted to transcend the ordinary and create lasting memories that become part of your personal legacy.
